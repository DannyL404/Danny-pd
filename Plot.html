Captain Holt arrives right at the start of the show and is the new captain of the precinct. This allows for the show to make sure that we are introduced to the characters properly as if we were him. The characters are all already close and have their own quirks that get shown as Captain Holt discovers them. Despite this we lean little about captain Holt because he is a very contained character. We start the journey of the show by seeing Amy and Jake having a highly competitive bet about who can arrest the most perps. The bet makes both the detectives act slightly out of regulations, especially Jake as he does not care for rules. Captain Holt arrives and is not happy about this and starts being very strict on the whole precinct, although on Jake the most as he is the least rule abiding. Despite this all the detectives respect him very quickly and follow his command almost immediately, apart from Jake initially but a few episodes in he became compliant. All the detectives start working together to make the Brooklyn area safer and less full of crime, and they are good as a team, making many arrests.
