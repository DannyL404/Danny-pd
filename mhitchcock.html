Michael Hitchcock


Detective Michael Hitchcock is one of the older male detectives at the precinct. He is a duo with Norm Scully and is just as lazy as him. Unlike the other main characters, he is referred to by his last name (and Scully as well). Hitchcock states that his job is doing nothing all day. He has been in the police for nearly 30 years and best friends with Norm Scully throughout most of it. The duo are together so often that they had been presumed to be a romantic couple at a party. The laziness is highlighted by his low arrest number in the last 12 months with 14 whilst other members of the squad have had 78. It is implied that he was once a much better and harder working detective, such as surviving being a police officer in the 1980s at a time when New York City was described as basically being a purge.
